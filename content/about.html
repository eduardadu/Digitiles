Generate digital mosaics
A platform to generate digital mosaics from different inputs. Upload an image, a video, use your webcam, or write with our custom fonts.
The platform provides you with ways to recycle these into different visual artefacts: a mosaic of digital tiles.
Check Instagram
Simple and fast process
Start by uploading an image (in PNG or JPG) or a video (in MOV or MP4) or use your webcam. You can also click Drawing Canvas to get a blank artboard. The system will then transform your creation into a mosaic.
You can choose from different tiles and mix them. Change colours, change the grid and other settings. Write using our fonts specially designed for the system or draw with tiles. In the end, export it the creation as a video (WEBM or GIF), an image (JPG, PNG, SVG).
Many applications
Since you can export your creations to different formats you can use them for multiple purposes, from posters, packaging, or other printed artefacts. You can also post the videos in social media, use them on your website or use them for marketing purposes.
